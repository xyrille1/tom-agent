Leaderboard Trial replay Methodology

Every model below is scored on a matched pair of conditions per scenario: a false-belief (F) trial, where the correct answer requires modeling what an NPC still believes despite the world having changed, and a true-belief control (T) trial with identical complexity. The ToM gap (T accuracy − F accuracy) isolates belief-modeling failure from scenario-comprehension failure. Every number here links to inspectable trials on the replay page.
